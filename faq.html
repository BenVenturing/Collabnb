Frequently asked questions

Collabnb is a two-sided marketplace that connects content creators — UGC filmmakers, micro-influencers, travel creators — with boutique hospitality hosts. Creators apply for stays in exchange for content deliverables. Hosts post collab listings to find and vet creators who'll tell their story. It's not an influencer agency and it's not a travel booking platform. It's direct collab infrastructure for both sides of the deal.

Airbnb is for paying guests. Collabnb is for collab arrangements where the stay is exchanged for content — there's no money changing hands for the room itself, just an agreement on deliverables. Unlike influencer platforms, we're not an agency taking a cut, we don't offer gifting campaigns for mass brands, and we don't cater to large hotel chains. We focus exclusively on the boutique end of hospitality and the creator-first end of travel content.

The first 100 verified creators and the first 100 verified hosts — 200 total founding members — will receive lifetime access to every Collabnb feature, regardless of any future pricing we introduce. "Verified" means our team has reviewed your application and approved it (manual audit, not automated). We track by submission date, so signing up early matters. Access is per-role: 100 creator spots and 100 host spots are separate pools.
