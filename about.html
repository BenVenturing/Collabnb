Our story

Collabnb is built for collabs,
not ads.

A two-sided marketplace where creators earn stays and hosts earn stories.

The collab economy already exists — it's just scattered across DMs, email threads, and half-baked spreadsheets. A boutique BnB in the Catskills wants a TikTok creator who fits their vibe. A UGC filmmaker in Bali wants a stay that's worth the content. Both sides know the deal they want. What they're missing is the marketplace to find each other.

Collabnb is that marketplace. We built it because the middle layer — discovery, agreement, trust, and follow-through — was missing. Not a travel platform. Not an influencer agency. A direct collab infrastructure for the boutique end of hospitality and the creator end of travel.

We're starting with a waitlist because the first 200 creators and 200 hosts will shape what this platform becomes. If you're early, you're in for life.
