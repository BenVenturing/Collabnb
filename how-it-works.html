From signup to first collab in three steps.

Simple on both sides.

Whether you're a creator looking for your next stay or a host looking for your next storyteller, the process is built to be fast and friction-free.

Creator track

For Creators

Get verified, browse stays, and start collabing. Your content is the currency.

1

Apply to the waitlist

Tell us your TikTok and Instagram handles, your creator tier, and how many collabs you've done in the last three months. It takes about two minutes. No lengthy applications, no agency gatekeeping.

UGC Beginner UGC Pro Micro Influencer
2

Get verified

Our team does a manual review of every creator — checking your content, your niche, and your fit for boutique hospitality collabs. This is how we keep quality high on both sides. The first 100 verified creators get lifetime access to Collabnb at launch.

3

Browse, apply, create, travel

Once the app launches June 1, you'll get access to a discovery feed of boutique stays actively looking for creators. Browse by location, property type, or deliverable style. Apply directly in the app, negotiate the scope, and go create.

Host track

For Hosts

Post a stay, set your terms, and get matched with the right creator for your brand.

1

Sign up as a host

Tell us about your property — the type, location, and what makes it interesting. We work with boutique hotels, BnBs, glamping sites, small resorts, curated experiences, and tourism boards. If you care about storytelling, you're a fit.

Boutique Hotel BnB Glamping Small Resort Experience
2

List your first collab stay

When the app launches, you'll post a collab listing: the stay details, the deliverable brief (e.g. 3 Reels + 1 TikTok), the length of stay, and any creative direction. You control the terms. Creators apply to you — not the other way around.

3

Review, approve, host

Review creator applications in the app — their portfolio, tier, past collabs, and fit for your property. Approve the best match, confirm the dates, and host them. After the stay, receive your deliverables. No agencies, no awkward cold outreach.

Why this exists

No more DM roulette.

Collabnb removes the awkward cold-outreach, the ghosting, the verbal agreements that fall apart, and the spreadsheet chaos. It puts the collab infrastructure in one place — and keeps both sides accountable.
